Business Problem Notebook experiments are easy. Productionizing ML reliably—across teams and environments—is the challenge. Why teams get stuck Many teams build models in notebooks (Colab/Jupyter) but struggle to productionize them. Models are trained manually, deployed inconsistently, hard to monitor or retrain, and lack governance and version control. Impact: delays, unreliable predictions, and operational inefficiencies. What this project solves This project delivers a repeatable, automated, and governed MLOps platform on GCP using Vertex AI—turning raw datasets into scalable prediction services with controlled promotion and cost-aware execution. Outcome: faster iteration with traceability, governance, and production readiness. What The Project Does Three modular pipelines automate experimentation, training with quality gates, and registry-driven deployment.
