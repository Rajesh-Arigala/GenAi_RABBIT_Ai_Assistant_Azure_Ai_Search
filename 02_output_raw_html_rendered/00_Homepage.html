My Journey

Engineering + management foundation from NITK and IIM Calcutta, refined through BPCL, Medtronic, SMAAT, and entrepreneurship, now applied to building practical, production-grade AI and GenAI systems for real-world business workflows.

I bring a foundation of engineering and business, combining systems thinking from the National Institute of Technology, Surathkal (NITK) with strategic depth from the Indian Institute of Management Calcutta (IIM Calcutta). Early experience at Bharat Petroleum Corporation Limited (BPCL) and Medtronic India exposed me to real-world operational systems where reliability, process discipline, and stakeholder alignment were critical.

I later extended this into infrastructure systems through my work with SMAAT India-Water, contributing to digitizing physical operations and bridging physical, data, and decision systems.

Building on this, I moved into entrepreneurship, creating RedRybbons and R-Cafe from the ground up—executing end-to-end systems across infrastructure, operations, and customer experience in highly unstructured environments.

This shaped my core strength: designing systems that work beyond theory.

Today, I focus on AI and GenAI platforms, building scalable, production-grade solutions aligned with real-world business workflows.
