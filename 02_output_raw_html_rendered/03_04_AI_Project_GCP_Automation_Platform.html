Enterprise AI Platform on GCP

End-to-End Vertex AI MLOps Pipelines for Production ML Deployment

Enterprise-grade MLOps platform on Google Cloud: built with Vertex AI, GitHub, and Google Colab. It automates the full ML lifecycle across modular pipelines — from experimentation to governed registry promotion and production deployment — with strong automation and cost control.

The demo use case is Wine Quality Prediction (regression/classification). The real value is the reusable platform pattern—adaptable for demand forecasting, pricing, churn, risk scoring, and operational optimization.

Business Problem

Notebook experiments are easy. Productionizing ML reliably—across teams and environments—is the challenge.

Why teams get stuck

Many teams build models in notebooks (Colab/Jupyter) but struggle to productionize them. Models are trained manually, deployed inconsistently, hard to monitor or retrain, and lack governance and version control.

Impact: delays, unreliable predictions, and operational inefficiencies.

What this project solves

This project delivers a repeatable, automated, and governed MLOps platform on GCP using Vertex AI—turning raw datasets into scalable prediction services with controlled promotion and cost-aware execution.

Outcome: faster iteration with traceability, governance, and production readiness.

Serverless-first design: Vertex AI manages compute automatically—training jobs provision on demand and terminate after completion; endpoints scale via replicas; batch prediction runs serverlessly with no persistent compute.

Cost-Aware GCP MLOps (FinOps)

Managed services keep costs aligned with usage: no idle training compute, on-demand endpoints, and batch prediction for low-cost workloads.

Cost controls implemented

  • Training jobs run only when triggered (auto-provision and terminate)
  • Endpoints can be deployed and deleted on demand
  • Batch prediction for cost-sensitive scheduled workloads
  • Cloud Storage for low-cost persistence of datasets/artifacts
  • GitHub-based workflows reduce infra overhead
Main cost driver: Vertex AI real-time endpoints. For demos/portfolio, delete endpoints when not in use.
